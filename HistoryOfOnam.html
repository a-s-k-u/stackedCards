The Origins

Onam (Malayalam: ഓണം, romanized: Ōṇaṁ) is an annual harvest festival celebrated in the Indian state of Kerala.A major annual event for Keralites, it is the official festival of the state[4][6] and includes a spectrum of cultural events.[7][8] Drawing from Hindu mythology,[4][9] Onam commemorates King Mahabali. Within the textual tradition (prim. Mahabharata).
